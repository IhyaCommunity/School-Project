New Muslim Public Hight School

New Muslim public high school is a non-profit organisation that has been set up by few locals in Burewala. The school was built in 1977 with the vision of educating children belonging to the lowest socioeconomic group of the community, the purpose of the school was to bring enlightenment in the matters of both cleen and duniya to these children and their families. The school has produced many skillful and well educated citizens that are now contributing towards the betterment of the society and striving to change the fate of thier families.

We can do it together lets do it now!

The school currently tutors 187 children from nursery to matriculation and is equipped with a highly decided staff that masters in both scientific studes as well as Hifz Tarjamah/Tafseer study of the Quran. What started of as a dream is now in the process of it's realisation. Every year more more students are being enrolled and but the facilities are only limited. We need your contribution in this beautiful cause to educate a child and through them an entire generation. We aspire to expand our program through Allah's help and your heartfelt donations to make sure that no child is deprived from the blessing of knowledge due to financial and social constraints.
